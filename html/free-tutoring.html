Free Tutoring at ECU

FREE tutoring is provided on campus by a variety of different departments. Specific hours and days tutoring services are provided do change from semester to semester. Students are encouraged to contact these departments for current information. ECU's Pirate Tutoring Center is very knowledgeable about tutoring resources on campus. Students may choose to begin with this office if they've never used tutoring resources before.

University Writing Center

At the University Writing Center students receive assistance with writing assignments for any academic course. Located in 1st floor of Joyner Library, students can drop in any time to meet individually with a consultant. Check the UWC website for hours and satellite locations on campus.
WEBSITE: http://www.ecu.edu/cs-acad/writing/writingcenter/.

Math Tutors

The Math Department offers a Math Tutor Center in the Pirate CAVE in the Joyner Library Basement for one on one help in MATH 0001, 0045, 1050, 1066, 1074, 1083, 1085, 2119, 2127 and Statistics: MATH 2228 and 2283. Stop by the Calculus Tutor Center in Pirate CAVE for one on one help in Calculus: MATH 2171, 2172 and 2173; Calculus for Life Sciences: MATH 2121 and 2122 and Engineering Calculus: MATH 2151, 2152 and 2153.
