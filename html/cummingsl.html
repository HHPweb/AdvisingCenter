Ms. Lauren Cummings

Lauren Cummings was born in Fayetteville, NC and grew up along the east coast (NC, SC, VA, PA and Washington DC).  She holds a Master of Science in Counselor Education as well as a Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education from East Carolina University.  She came to the College of Health and Human Performance Advising Center in November 2010.  Prior to advising, she worked as an Elementary School Counselor in Martin County Schools, NC.  She has a passion for working with students and hopes to make a positive impact on their success at ECU!

  • Member of American Counseling Association
  • Member of Chi Sigma Iota (Counseling Academic and Professional Honor Society International)
  • Member of Kappa Delta Pi (Honor Society for Education)
  • Member of Golden Key International Honor Society
  • Member of Phi Delta Kappa (Honor Society)
  • Member of the National Residence Hall Honorary
  • North Carolina Licensed Teacher
  • COAD 1000 Instructor
