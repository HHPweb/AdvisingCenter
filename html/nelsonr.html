Ms. Robbin Nelson

Robbin Herring Nelson grew up in Tomahawk, NC (Sampson County).  She came to work with The College of Health and Human Performance Advising Center in February, 2007.  Robbin started working with ECU in 1986 in the English Department as an office assistant.  After taking time off to raise her children, she returned working with ECU at ECU Cardiology as a patient representative and also with ECU Speech, Language, and Hearing Clinic as an office assistant.

Robbin brings to the Advising Center many years of experience working as a team member with faculty, staff, and students.
