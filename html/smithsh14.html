Ms. Sharon Smith

Sharon Smith is a native of Pitt County, NC graduating from D. H. Conley High School in 2000. Sharon hold a Master of Science in Higher Education Administration from Drexel University and a Bachelor of Arts degree in English Literature from North Carolina Central University. She is currently working to complete her doctoral degree in Educational Leadership at East Carolina University. She joined the HHP advising team on April 1, 2014. Prior to advising, Sharon worked as a social research assistant for the North Carolina Community College System Office where she worked for several years increasing her skills and knowledge in a range of educational matters including policy research, administration practices, grant funding procedures, student development, curriculum courses, academic programs of study and leadership skills. Her passion for holistic student development through advising practices pushes her to try to be the very best advisor she can be for every student she encounters both in and out of her office.
