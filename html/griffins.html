Ms. Sandie Griffin

Sandie Griffin recently relocated to Greenville, NC form Blacksburg, VA.  She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Elementary Education from Virginia Tech and a Master of Science in Academic Advising from Kansas State University.  Before joining the HHP Advising staff in June 2011 Ms. Griffin taught language arts in middle school, worked in the Admissions Office at Radford University, was an assistant counselor in the Office of Scholarships & Financial Aid at Virginia Tech and an undergraduate enrollment specialist and undergraduate advisor in the Engineering Science and Mechanics Department at Virginia Tech.

  • Member of Phi Kappa Phi
