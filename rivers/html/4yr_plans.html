Rivers Advising Center Four Year Plans

The four-year plans listed below are a suggested way for freshmen in a program to complete their major in four years. All students should use the four-year plan as a guide for sequencing their required courses. The academic advisors will assist, but managing the curriculum is ultimately the responsibility of the student. Students can use the four-year plan, check sheet and the ECU catalog for information on sequencing and pre-requisite requirements for classes. (Please note, that due to staffing and budgetary concerns, there may be a variation in the term that a course is offered. However, with adequate planning and communication with the advisor, this should not delay graduation.)
