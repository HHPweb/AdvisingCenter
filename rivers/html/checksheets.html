Rivers Advising Center Check Sheets

A check sheet outlines all requirements for a degree according to different categories (such as Foundation Curriculum, major, minor, etc.). It helps you and your advisor keep track of classes completed. Be sure to select the check sheet for the correct major and catalog year. Update your check sheet after each semester and bring it to your advisor meetings. Use the check sheet and four year plan to help you plan the sequence of classes.
