Rivers Advising Center Requirements to Declare a Major

If you have met the criteria listed below for your intended major, then you should email or meet with your academic advisor. He/she will make sure you have met the requirements and will help you declare your major. To view the criteria, click your intended major below.

  • Child Development & Family Relations
  • Interior Design
  • Merchandising
  • Social Work
  • Birth through Kindergarten (BK)
    • 2.5 cumulative GPA
  • Family & Consumer Sciences Teacher Education (FACS)
    • 2.5 cumulative GPA
  • Family and Community Services (FCS)
    • 2.5 cumulative GPA
    • Application is required
  • Child Life (CL)
    • 3.0 cumulative GPA
    • Essay is required.
Prior to 2010 catalog
  • IDSN 1180, 1181, 1281, 1500 (minimum of "C").
    • Minimum overall GPA of 2.5 required.
  • Courses required before portfolio review: IDSN 1180, 1181, 1281, 1500, 2040, 2800, 2850 and ART 1905.
    • Minimum grade of "C" required in all IDSN courses.
    • 2.5 overall GPA to submit portfolio.

IDSN 2700, 2750 and 2040 are not required for portfolio review but it is recommended that they are taken before. Often there are schedule conflicts with professional level classes if these are taken after the review.

Beginning 2010-2011 catalog
  • Courses required to declare and before portfolio review: IDSN 1180, 1181, 1281, 1500, 2800 and ART 1905.
    • Minimum grade of "C" required in all IDSN courses.
    • 2.5 Overall GPA to submit portfolio.

IDSN 2700, 2750 and 2040 are not required for portfolio review but it is recommended that they are taken before. Often there are schedule conflicts with professional level classes if these are taken after the review.

Prior to 2006-2007 catalog
  • MRCH 1135, Math 1065 with a minimum of "C" or better
  • 2.5 overall GPA
2006-2010 catalog
  • MRCH 1135, 2239 or 1180, 2350 and 2999 with a minimum of "C" or better
  • MATH 1065 with a minimum of "C" or better
  • 2.5 overall GPA
Beginning 2010-2011 catalog
  • MRCH 1135, 2239 or 1180, and 2350 with a minimum of "C" or better
  • MATH 1065 with a minimum of "C" or better
  • 2.5 overall GPA
  • Complete at least 32 hours
  • 2.5 overall GPA
  • SOCW 1010 and 2010 with a 2.5 GPA in these courses.

Application-School only accepts students into the program in the fall semester.
